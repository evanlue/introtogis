    Welcome to Intro to GIS!

    This site will be used mainly to host lecture slides.

    Textbooks

    The lectures synchronize with chapters in Bolstad (2012). See book list below. Bolstad, P. 2012. GIS Fundamentals: A First Text on Geographic Information Systems, 4th Edition. White Bear Lake, MN: Eider Press.
