Spatial Prediction

Different from general estimation in that a dependent variable is predicted from independent variables.

Spatial Autocorrelation

Bolstad 2012, Fig 12.12

  • How values vary in close proximity
  • The first law of geography (created by Waldo Tobler) states that all things are related, but closer things are more related.

Cross Correlation

Same as a correlation between two variables

Bolstad 2012, Fig 12.12

Moran's I

Bolstad 2012, Eq 12.3

  • A measure of spatial autocorrelation.
  • Ranges from -1 to 1, where -1 represents strong anti-correlation, 0 represents no correlation, and 1 represents strong positive correlation.
  • You obviously don't need to memorize this formula.
