Cartographic Models

  • Overlay: buffers, reclassification

  • Mathematical functions
  • Outputs are interval or ratio
  • Often predictive
  • Spatio-Temporal Models include time

Spatio-Temporal Models

Features of Spatio-Temporal Models

  • 3 to 4 dimensions: XY and T (sometimes a Z)
  • Locations of things may stay the same (e.g. demographic analysis) or change (e.g. forest stands after fires)
  • Deterministic models - outputs are always the same
  • Stochastic models - introduces randomness into the model; outputs will vary slightly based on parameters are processes that are known to be unpredictable
